What Should I Have For Dinner? Application

This is a project was created in the year of 2025. The purpose of this application is to help you choose what to make based on how much time you have or how long it would take to make it.

How this application works is you drag the button to choose your time and you pick any of the options in the dropdown, vice versa. In the dropdown, behind each element there is a list of foods of each catagory. For example, in the Soup catagory there is a list of Wonton Noodle Soup, Broccoli Cheddar Soup, and Egg Drop Soup. What determines what you get out of the three depends on the time you choose. To get Wonton Noodle Soup you would have you set you time 60 minutes or less, Broccoli Cheddar Soup would be 30 minutes or less, and Egg Drop Soup would be 5 minutes or less. However, if you wanted Wonton Noodle Soup but only had twenty two minutes that wouldn't work and you would get Broccoli Cheddar Soup.
